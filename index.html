Relaxation Techniques

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4s, hold for 7s, exhale for 8s. Repeat 4 times.

Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)

Ackowledge 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense and then relax each muscle group, starting from your toes up to your head.
